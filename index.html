About Me

Hey there, welcome to my portfolio page. If you've stumbled upon this link by chance or are here to know more about me, allow me to introduce myself!
I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering and Engineering Science at UNC Charlotte. My research revolves around speeding up macroscale simulations in continuum mechanics using machine learning techniques. Additionally, I like studying inverse problems with a special interest in solving them using deep learning. I thoroughly enjoy working on problems at the intersection of numerical analysis and programming, and I take great pleasure in implementing algorithms. Furthermore, I am currently serving as a Ph.D. intern at Reactor Materials and Mechanical Design Group, Pacific Northwest National Laboratory. Before this, I devoted a significant number of years to academia in India.
To learn more about me and my work, feel free to explore the other sections of this website.
Thank you for visiting!"
